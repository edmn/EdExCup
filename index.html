Rules...

  1. Entrants pay 2 pounds in per week, 1 pound to weekly competition and 1 pound into the overall pot. The overall pot will be paid to the winner on the last completion which will be the last Saturday before Christmas.
  2. Each weeks competition will be stableford. When 18 holes is not open it will be calculated pro rata over '18' holes.
  3. The last competition on the last Saturday before Christmas will be double points with the score over the pro rata '18' holes doubled.
  4. Entrants must be in attendance in the pro shop (through the door) at the alotted time which will be set pre midnight on the Friday night before. The alotted time will be exact to the second. I.e 1030 will be 10.30.00.
  5. In the event of a breach of 4. Above a point will be deducted in 5 minute bands for lateness for worked to the exact second I.e 10.30.01 -10.35.00 and so on and so forth.
  6. Participants must declare entrance before midnight on Friday night of each weeks competition.
  7. 'The Stewart Rule' following declared entrance failure to turn up the next day will result in being assigned 0 points for the day. "Non attendance" is defined as being 15 minutes late from the alotted start time and all players have played the tee.
  8. Without prejudice to 7. above if someone declares they can not be attend on a particular week they will be assigned 29 points save in the event that the winning score that week is less that the non attendance score then non attendance score that week will be one less than the winning score.
  9. The rules will be enforced by a majority vote between the committee members of Martin, Harkin and McBurney.
  10. One appeal permitted per penalty determined on basis of 9 above. The result of the appeal is final.
  11. 'Waifs and Strays Rule'. Non founding members may play on a weekly basis and pay in pound for weekly comp. However, they are prohibited from winning the Ed Ex unless they ask to play in Ed Ex before the 1st week.
